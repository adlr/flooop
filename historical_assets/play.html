Instructions: You are the circle (the Flooop). Your goal is to get to the top row by pushing blocks around until you get there. Use the arrow keys to move. See below for the behavior of the various pieces you will encounter.

Pieces and their behavior.
In general you can push any piece with agreeing arrows. If you have a string of aligned and touching pieces and you push one end, the force is transferred through and the last one moves.
